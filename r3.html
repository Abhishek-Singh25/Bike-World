Slim and lightweight sports chassis

The compact and lightweight chassis is manufactured with latest structural analysis technology that gives an optimum balance of strength and rigidity. The near 50/50 front/rear weight distribution and accessible seat height deliver a confident and balanced ride.

37 mm upside down front forks

Upside-down front fork with 37mm inner tubes provides good front-end feel during cornering, braking and other riding conditions. This design gives a sporty front-end feel and feedback without losing real-world comfort.

Advanced Fully Digital LCD Meter Console

The R3 features multi-function LCD meter console that display’s information on gear position, fuel capacity, real time and average fuel economy. There’s also a water temperature gauge, clock, oil change indicator and trip meter – all presented in a clean, modern layout.

LOW FUEL TANK AND HANDLEBAR POSITION

Thanks to the low fuel tank design and low handlebars, the R3's ergonomics give a pure supersport riding experience. As well as being extremely comfortable, the riding position makes it easy to grip the machine with your knees for improved confidence, especially when you’re tucking in close to the bodywork for maximum aerodynamic efficiency.

LED FLASHERS - FRONT & REAR

Signal your unique identity with unmissable front and back LED flashers – a perfect mash-up of style and sporty.

YZR-M1 INSPIRED CAST ALUMINIUM HANDLEBAR CROWN

The cast aluminium handlebar crown creates an exciting view as if sitting on the M1.The design with reinforcement ribs contributes to handling and steering that feels natural.
